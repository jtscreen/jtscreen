Joshua Screen | jtscreen

Joshua Screen is a multidisaplinary artist with a focus on acting, music production, photography and web design.

As an actor, he has appeared in various projects in both film and stage. With nearly a decade of experience in the art, he would love to be part of any project presented to him with other passionate artists.

As a music producer, Joshua Screen (who often goes by jtscreen on his own productions) started out by producing his own song writing. He has worked on some independednt artists work and has even worked on some original music for a feature film.

As a photographer, Joshua primarily focuses on headshots, portrature, and production photos for performances.

As a web designer, Joshua has a fare amount of experience. Before his beginnings in the arts, he had a background in computer science. He even created this website from scratch.
